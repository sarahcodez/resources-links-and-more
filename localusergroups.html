  • Hack Night SLC - http://www.hacknightslc.com/
    For those of you who haven't made it out to one of our evens yet... Hack Night SLC is sort of a mobile co-working space. Once a month we take over part of a local companies space, provide food, internet, and door prizes and turn you lose to work on building your dream projects. You know, the ones that you alway talk about building but never seem to find the time to actually build. Yeah those projects. Now you can come work on them while hanging out with some of the best developers and designers in Utah.
    TLDR; Free food. Door Prizes. Smart talented people. Time to code your projects. Register: http://hacknightslc.com
